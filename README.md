## Process

This assessment is completed in **two passes**, in a proctored environment.

1. **Fork** this repository before starting
2. Use the **10-minute reading period** to review all sections and plan your approach
3. Complete **Pass 1**, then **Pass 2**

### Pass 1 — No AI Tooling
Complete as much as you can using only your own knowledge and official documentation. You may skip questions you get stuck on. AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Copilot, etc.) are **not permitted** during this pass.

### Pass 2 — With AI Tooling
Return to your answers and complete anything you skipped or want to improve. AI tools are permitted and encouraged during this pass.

Each section folder contains separate `no-ai/` and `ai/` subfolders for your answers.

## Proctoring and Recording Requirements

This assessment is supervised in-person at the office. The following requirements apply:

1. **Single screen only** — you are limited to one screen for the duration of the assessment
2. **Google Meet session** — set up a Google Meet call before starting and record the entire session (both passes)
3. **Screen recording** — your screen must be shared and recorded throughout. Recordings will be reviewed and assessed (automated)
4. **No external communication** — do not communicate with others during the assessment

Failure to comply with proctoring requirements may result in disqualification.
